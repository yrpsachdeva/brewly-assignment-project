ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Born in a Garage. Brewed with Passion.

Brewly started in 2021 when two coffee-obsessed friends decided the world deserved better than bitter, over-roasted beans shipped weeks old. We source micro-lot harvests directly from farms in Coorg, Chikmagalur, and Araku Valley — then roast every batch in small quantities to lock in peak flavour.

Our single-origin cold brews and pour-over blends are crafted for people who believe morning coffee should be a ritual, not a routine. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just exceptional coffee, delivered fresh to your door.
